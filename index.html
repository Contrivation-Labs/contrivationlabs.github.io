Who We Are

Contrivation Labs

Contrivation Labs is a Bangalore-based Agritech start-up, incorporated in February 2019. We are focused on developing innovative Harvest and Post-Harvest Solutions for the Indian Agriculture sector.

Our ideas germinate to find solutions for the Indian Farmer and Agriculture Eco-system, but are equally suited for Global Markets.

Our revenue model is based on a hybrid mix of FaaS (Farming-as-a-Service) and B2B sales, enabling flexible adoption for enterprises and individual farm operators alike.

Our Products

CalyXtract®

Automated Chili Calyx & Stem Separation System

CalyXtract is the first product designed and developed by Contrivation Labs. It is a fully automated solution to separate the Calyx and Stem from Chili pods without damaging the pods from stress caused by temperature, mechanical stress, or harsh lighting. This preserves quality throughout the post-harvest process, enabling operators to command premium pricing while dramatically cutting labour costs.
